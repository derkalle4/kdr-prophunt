    welcome to prop hunt

    [F1] to hide

    in this game mode you join either seeker or hider:

    seeker

    search and kill all hider. Be careful: you loose health when shooting at something else then a hidden prop.

    hider

    hide yourself by changing your model to something available on the map. Smaller objects have less health then bigger objects.

    The game will start when 2 or more players are connected. Please wait for a new round. You will join automatically.

    work in progress

    this game mode is in alpha status. feel free to discuss it on our discord (discord.kandru.net) or directly in this ingame chat. it will be made open source after the modding competition in mid january. balancing can be an issue at this time being. please bare with us as we make progress in optimization and features.
