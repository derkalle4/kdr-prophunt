    welcome to prop hunt

    In this game mode you join either seeker or hider.

    seeker

    Search and kill all hider. Be careful: you loose health when shooting at something else then a hidden prop.

    hider

    Hide yourself by changing your model to something available on the map. Smaller objects have less health then bigger objects.

    The game will automatically start when 2 or more players are connected. You cannot join during an round and will automatically join next round.
