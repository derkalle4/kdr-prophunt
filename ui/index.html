    welcome to prop hunt

    In this game mode you join either Seekers or Hiders. Seekers need to chase and kill Hiders but loose health every time they shoot. If they kill a Hider they will regain health. Hiders need to change their look by selecting a prop from the map and hide as good as possible to win after the round is over.

    The game will automatically start when 2 or more players are connected. You cannot join during an round and will automatically join next round.
